About CEG

The College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG) is among the oldest institutions for technical education in the country. Over the decades, it has evolved into a premier engineering campus that combines strong classroom learning with practical, research-oriented education.

CEG functions under Anna University and attracts students from across Tamil Nadu and India. The institution emphasizes discipline, academic rigor, innovation, and social responsibility. Students are encouraged to pursue interdisciplinary learning and participate in projects that address societal and industrial needs.

Institutional Strengths

CEG's strengths include experienced faculty, established laboratories, active student chapters of professional bodies, and a wide alumni base that contributes to mentoring, placements, and research collaboration. The campus culture supports both technical excellence and personal growth.
