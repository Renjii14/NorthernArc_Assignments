College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG)

The College of Engineering, Guindy is one of India's historic engineering institutions and is a constituent campus of Anna University, Chennai. It is widely recognized for its long academic legacy, highly qualified faculty, and contribution to engineering education, research, and innovation.

CEG provides a vibrant learning environment where students build strong technical foundations and practical skills through laboratories, projects, internships, and industry interactions. The campus hosts active technical societies, cultural clubs, sports teams, and entrepreneurship initiatives that support holistic student development.

Campus Highlights

  • Academic programs across core engineering, technology, and architecture disciplines.
  • Research centers and laboratories focused on real-world engineering challenges.
  • Strong alumni network with global presence in industry, research, and public service.
  • Regular technical symposiums, workshops, hackathons, and innovation events.
