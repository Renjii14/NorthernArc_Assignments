Departments at CEG

CEG offers a broad range of departments that support undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral studies. Each department combines theory, laboratory work, and project-based learning to build strong engineering competence.

Major Academic Areas

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Information Science and Technology
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Printing and Packaging Technology
  • Architecture and Planning

Departments regularly organize seminars, technical guest lectures, mini-project reviews, and collaborative research to keep students aligned with current technologies and industry practices.
