Admissions

Admissions to programs at CEG are conducted through established state and national level procedures. Candidates should follow the official Anna University and Government counseling notifications for current eligibility rules, cut-off details, and document requirements.

Typical Admission Routes

  • Undergraduate programs through centralized engineering counseling processes.
  • Postgraduate programs based on qualifying examinations and counseling/allotment.
  • Research admissions through university-announced procedures, interviews, and eligibility screening.

Applicants are advised to prepare academic records, community certificates (if applicable), transfer certificates, and identity documents in advance to ensure a smooth admission process.
