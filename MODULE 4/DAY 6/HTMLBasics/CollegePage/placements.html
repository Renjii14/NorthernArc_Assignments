Placements

CEG has a strong placement ecosystem supported by a dedicated placement office, faculty coordinators, and active alumni engagement. Students are trained through aptitude sessions, resume workshops, mock interviews, and coding practice to improve employability.

Placement Support

  • Pre-placement training in communication, aptitude, and interview readiness.
  • Campus recruitment drives by companies from core engineering, IT, analytics, and consulting sectors.
  • Internship opportunities that often lead to full-time offers.
  • Alumni mentoring and guidance for higher studies and career planning.

Students are encouraged to build projects, participate in technical events, and maintain a strong academic profile to maximize placement outcomes.
